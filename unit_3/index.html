Hi! I'm Amelia. It's nice to meet you! ♥

I am a UX Product Designer based in Boston. When I'm not working or teaching yoga, I seek environments and experiences that encourage my intuition, sensitivity, imagination and creativity.

I have a deep respect for the human condition and love to look at design as a means of directing energy to effectively disrupt what is out of date and in need of innovation.
