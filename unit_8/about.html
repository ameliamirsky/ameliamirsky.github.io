Amelia is a UX Product Designer at
Ellevation Education in Boston.

Since starting 4 years ago, she has had the opportunity to contribute in several roles; starting in Product Support, transitioning to Quality Assurance, and now most recently in Product Design.

She has a deep respect for the human condition, and looks at design as a means of directing energy into restoring what is out of date or in need of innovation.

When she's not working or teaching yoga, she's probably cooking, eating, or seeking environments and experiences that encourage her intuition, imagination and creativity.

She loves to collaborate and chat over a good cup of coffee - so feel free to get in touch or recommend a spot.
