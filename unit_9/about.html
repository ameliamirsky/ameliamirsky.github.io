Nice to meet you.

I'm a UX designer based in Boston, currently working at Ellevation Education. We partner with school districts who use our products to manage complex program requirements, build the capacity of their teachers and help English Learners reach their highest aspirations. I have had the opportunity to contribute in several roles at Ellevation; starting in Product Support, transitioning to Quality Assurance, and now most recently in Product Design.

I have a deep respect for the human condition, and I'm energized by exploring new mediums and industries — from social good to emerging technologies — to learn how design can be leveraged in order to restore what is out of date or in need of innovation.

In my spare time, I teach yoga, travel as much as I can, and try to seek environments and experiences that encourage my intuition, imagination and creativity. I love to collaborate or connect over a good cup of coffee - so please feel free to get in touch or recommend a spot!
